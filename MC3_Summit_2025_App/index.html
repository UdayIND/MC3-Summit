What This Tells Us About Our Community

Child poverty rates in Monroe County have shown remarkable improvement since 2012, demonstrating the effectiveness of coordinated community efforts. This progress reflects our collective commitment to ensuring every child has the conditions they need to thrive.

  • Community Impact: 8.3 percentage point reduction in child poverty
  • Geographic Equity: Progress seen across urban and rural areas
  • Critical Success Factor: Integration of education, health, and social services
