Social Services Priorities

Strategic priorities for strengthening the social safety net and community support systems for Monroe County families and children.

Integrated Services

Coordinate and streamline access to multiple support services through integrated service delivery models.

Early Intervention

Identify and address family needs early to prevent crises and reduce the need for intensive interventions.

Evidence-Based Practice

Implement and evaluate programs based on proven strategies that improve outcomes for children and families.

Community Partnership

Strengthen partnerships between public agencies, nonprofits, and community organizations to maximize resources.
