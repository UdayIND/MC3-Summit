Economic Development Priorities

Strategic priorities for strengthening economic opportunities and reducing barriers to family economic stability in Monroe County.

Job Growth

Attract and develop businesses that provide good-paying jobs with opportunities for career advancement.

Housing Solutions

Increase affordable housing options and support homeownership opportunities for working families.

Skills Development

Provide workforce training and education aligned with local economic opportunities and industry needs.

Economic Equity

Ensure economic opportunities are accessible to all community members, reducing systemic barriers to prosperity.
