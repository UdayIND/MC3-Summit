Based on data analysis and community input, these priorities will guide educational investments and policy decisions for Monroe County children and youth.

Early Intervention

Strengthen early childhood education and intervention programs to ensure all children start kindergarten ready to learn.

Career Readiness

Prepare students for future careers through enhanced STEM education, vocational training, and life skills development.

Strengthen partnerships between schools, families, and community organizations to support student success.
